Scam Alert

Fraudulent Google Business Account

It has come to our attention that fake business account are being created on google by someone claiming to provide delivery service and receiving online payments.

We wish to inform the general public that Papaye Fast Foods has not authorized anyone to transact online business on our behalf. Such business account should therefore be disregarded

The general public is here by notified that:
  • Currently we have not assigned any person or persons to operate delivery service on our behalf
  • Orders are made over the counter in our restaurants
  • Payment for orders using Mobile money and other electronic means are ONLY made over the counter in our restaurants and a corresponding receipt issued instantly to the customer.
